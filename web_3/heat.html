Green Rooftop

Reducing heat islands
Heat Islands: Is an urban region that is warmer than its surrounding rual areas. This is caused by high use of energy from people, cars, buses and trains in big cities.


Green rooftops on building can be upto 5 degrees cooler than those of conventional roofs. This collectivley within a city can make a big difference. Green rooftops can also reduce building energy use by 0.7%, due to less interior cooling needed, leading to an annual savings of $0.23 per square foot of roof's surface.

  • Benifits:

  • Reduced air pollution: Vegitation lowers air-conditioning demands, and can also remove pollutants and GHGs from the air through dry depositionand carbon sequestration and storage, respectively.

  • Enhanced stormwater management and water Quality: Green roofs absorb rainwater and slow stormwater runoff in the urban environment. It also filters pollutants from rainfall. Reducing pollutants that end up in the stormdrains and furthermore the ocean.
