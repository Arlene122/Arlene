George Street 2020

Why Emerald Greenway is an Urban Transformation

  • George Street 2020 aims to make travel throughout the city; safer, more efficient, and an overall more comfortable environment for people within the city.

  • The pedestrianisation of Gerorge Street will improve local store sales, and overall improving the local economy.

Diagrams of the Transforamtion

Light Rail 'Spine'

The implementation of the light rail "spine aims to create greater connectivity within Sydney. The light rail will connect 3 main squares creating easy navigation throughout the city. To coordinate other forms of transport, 'TFNSW' is devloping plans to change bus and road traffic networks to intergrate with the light rail. This will maintain eficiency within the heart of the city.

Pedestrian Focus

The vision is to create a street that is closed to traffic, making it a thriving pedestrian environment. This will reduce noise and congestion in the area, making an environment more confortable to be in.

Transformation

The street design of george street will take on a major transforation. The plan also introduces trees to the area to create shade and a greener atmosphere. The pedestrian area will support retail and local economy. The pedestrian street will include outdoor activities such as; outdoor dining, creative and cultural activies, to create a more vibrate environment.

Light Rail Conopy

The light rail stop will incorporate a canopy, to provide shelter for for up to 100 people (1 thrid of a full light rail vehicle). The design will complement building awnings, street trees within the city.
